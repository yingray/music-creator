## v5 Metatags Demo

This prompt demonstrates the **hybrid approach** — style prompt sets global sound, metatags in the lyrics field control per-section behavior.

### Why These Terms

- **108 BPM** — sits in the indie pop sweet spot (102–112 range from genre-BPM calibration)
- **"warm female vocal"** — v5 vocal direction term; more specific than just "female vocals"
- **"content creation background"** — use case helps Suno aim for the right energy level and mixability
- **"airy synth pad"** — texture adjective + instrument for clarity

### Metatags Lyrics Example

```
[Intro]
[Acoustic Guitar]
[Atmosphere: Dreamy]

[Verse 1]
[Vocal Style: Whisper]
Walking through the morning light
Every shadow fades away
Colors bleeding through the trees
Another ordinary day

[Pre-Chorus]
[Energy: Building]
But something's changing in the air
Can you feel it everywhere

[Chorus]
[Belted]
[Energy: Explosive]
We are golden we are free
Nothing's gonna hold us down
Every step is a melody
Dancing on familiar ground

[Verse 2]
[Vocal Style: Breathless]
Midnight conversations stars above
Every word a universe
Collecting moments made of love
Written in a silent verse

[Bridge]
[Mood: Nostalgic]
[Harmonies]
Remember when we started here
When everything was crystal clear

[Outro: Fade out]
[Callback: Chorus melody]
```

### Tips

- The `[Vocal Style: Whisper]` → `[Belted]` progression creates dynamic contrast between verse and chorus
- `[Energy: Building]` on the pre-chorus naturally leads into the explosive chorus
- `[Callback: Chorus melody]` at the outro makes the ending feel cohesive
- v5 maintains vocal persona consistency — the "warm" character stays even across different delivery styles

### Variations to Try

1. **More intimate**: Drop BPM to 96, change use case to "late night playlist", add `[Effect: Lo-fi]`
2. **More energetic**: Push BPM to 112, add "bright crisp production" to style prompt
3. **Male vocal version**: Change to "warm tenor vocal", keep everything else — v5 adapts well
