## Synthwave Retro Build

Demonstrates **era anchoring** ("late 80s") and v5's improved handling of temporal references.

### Why These Terms

- **96 BPM** — mid-range for synthwave (84–108 calibrated range); slow enough for night-drive feel
- **"late 80s analog synth"** — temporal reference is more effective than naming artists in v5
- **"Juno pads"** — specific synth model reference adds character (Roland Juno-106 is iconic synthwave)
- **"gated snares"** — period-accurate production term; signature 80s sound
- **"soft dream-pop vocal"** — combines vocal direction with sub-genre hint

### Production Notes

- **`[Effect: Reverb: Hall]`** on intro sets the spacious synthwave tone immediately
- **`[Synthesizer Solo]`** in the instrumental break is a synthwave genre staple
- **`[Effect: Radio Filter]`** on the bridge creates a "memory" effect — like hearing through old speakers
- **`[Texture: Grainy]`** on outro adds analog warmth to the fade

### Variations to Try

1. **Faster energy**: Push to 108 BPM, remove "night-drive", add "neon-lit, high energy"
2. **Instrumental version**: Drop vocal terms, add `[Instrumental]` tags, focus on synth layers
3. **Darker tone**: Change mood to "dark", add "minor key" to style prompt, use `[Mood: Dark]` metatags
