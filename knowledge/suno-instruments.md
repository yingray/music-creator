## Tips for Instrument Prompts
- Specify playing style: "fingerpicking guitar" > "guitar"
- Add adjectives: "warm bass", "distorted guitar", "ethereal pad"
- Mention specific models for character: "Rhodes piano", "Moog synthesizer"
- Use ensemble terms for layered sound: "string section", "brass ensemble"
- Combine texture + instrument: "reverb-drenched piano", "lo-fi drum machine"
