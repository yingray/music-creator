# Suno Genre Reference

## Electronic
- **Ambient**: ethereal, drone, atmospheric, space ambient, dark ambient
- **EDM**: house, techno, trance, dubstep, drum and bass, hardstyle
- **Synthwave**: retrowave, darksynth, outrun, vaporwave, chillwave
- **Downtempo**: trip-hop, chillout, lounge, nu-jazz electronic
- **Experimental Electronic**: glitch, IDM, noise, industrial electronic

## Rock
- **Classic Rock**: blues rock, hard rock, psychedelic rock, southern rock
- **Alternative**: indie rock, post-punk, shoegaze, grunge, britpop
- **Metal**: heavy metal, thrash metal, death metal, black metal, doom metal, progressive metal
- **Post-Rock**: atmospheric, crescendo-driven, ambient rock
- **Punk**: punk rock, hardcore punk, pop punk, post-hardcore

## Hip-Hop / Rap
- **Boom Bap**: classic hip-hop, 90s style, sample-based
- **Trap**: 808 bass, hi-hats, dark trap, melodic trap
- **Lofi Hip-Hop**: lofi beats, study beats, jazzy hip-hop
- **Conscious Hip-Hop**: lyrical, storytelling, socially aware
- **Phonk**: Memphis rap inspired, cowbell, chopped vocals

## Jazz
- **Traditional Jazz**: swing, bebop, dixieland, big band
- **Modern Jazz**: contemporary jazz, fusion, smooth jazz
- **Free Jazz**: avant-garde, experimental jazz, free improvisation
- **Latin Jazz**: bossa nova, Afro-Cuban jazz, samba jazz

## Classical
- **Orchestral**: symphonic, chamber orchestra, film score
- **Chamber Music**: string quartet, piano trio, woodwind quintet
- **Solo Instrument**: solo piano, solo violin, solo guitar
- **Choral**: sacred choral, a cappella, requiem
- **Contemporary Classical**: minimalist, neo-romantic, spectral

## R&B / Soul
- **Classic Soul**: Motown, Northern soul, Memphis soul
- **Neo-Soul**: modern soul, alternative R&B
- **Contemporary R&B**: pop R&B, trap soul
- **Funk**: classic funk, p-funk, electro-funk

## Country
- **Traditional Country**: honky-tonk, bluegrass, outlaw country
- **Modern Country**: country pop, bro-country, Americana
- **Country Folk**: folk country, alt-country, Appalachian

## Folk / Acoustic
- **Folk**: traditional folk, contemporary folk, indie folk
- **Singer-Songwriter**: acoustic, intimate, confessional
- **World Folk**: Celtic, Balkan, African folk, Asian folk

## Pop
- **Mainstream Pop**: dance pop, synth pop, electropop
- **Indie Pop**: dream pop, chamber pop, twee pop
- **K-Pop / J-Pop**: Korean pop, Japanese pop, city pop
- **Art Pop**: experimental pop, avant-pop

## World / Ethnic
- **Latin**: reggaeton, salsa, cumbia, bachata, bolero
- **African**: afrobeat, highlife, soukous, amapiano
- **Middle Eastern**: Arabic, Turkish, Persian, oud-based
- **Asian**: Indian classical, Chinese traditional, gamelan, koto
- **Caribbean**: reggae, dancehall, calypso, soca

## Cinematic / Score
- **Film Score**: orchestral score, thriller score, action score
- **Trailer Music**: epic trailer, hybrid orchestral, cinematic percussion
- **Game Music**: 8-bit, chiptune, RPG score, action game
- **Ambient Score**: soundscape, atmospheric score, environmental

## Prompt Tips for Genre
- Be specific with sub-genres: "dark ambient" > "ambient"
- Combine genres for unique results: "jazz-infused electronic", "orchestral metal"
- Reference era for style: "70s funk", "90s hip-hop", "80s synthwave"
- Use "fusion" or "influenced" for blends: "jazz-fusion", "African-influenced electronic"
