# Suno Mood & Energy Reference

## Mood Categories

### Positive / Bright
| Mood | Description | Good with |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Uplifting | Inspiring, hopeful, rising energy | Pop, EDM, orchestral |
| Happy | Joyful, cheerful, carefree | Pop, funk, reggae |
| Euphoric | Ecstatic, peak energy, blissful | Trance, EDM, gospel |
| Playful | Fun, whimsical, lighthearted | Pop, jazz, chiptune |
| Warm | Cozy, comforting, nostalgic | Acoustic, soul, lofi |

| Mood | Description | Good with |

## Energy Levels

| Level | BPM Range | Description | Genres |
|-------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Very Low | 40-60 | Ambient, meditative, still | Ambient, drone |
| Low | 60-80 | Slow, gentle, relaxed | Ballad, downtempo |
| Medium-Low | 80-100 | Moderate, walking pace | Lofi, R&B, soul |
| Medium | 100-120 | Steady, comfortable | Pop, rock, house |
| Medium-High | 120-140 | Upbeat, driving | Dance, techno, trance |
| High | 140-160 | Fast, intense, urgent | Drum and bass, punk |
| Very High | 160+ | Extreme, frantic | Hardcore, speedcore |

## Combining Moods
- Use 2-3 mood descriptors max to avoid confusion
- Pair contrasting moods for interesting results: "melancholic yet beautiful", "dark but uplifting"
- Energy + mood is more effective than mood alone: "energetic and euphoric" > just "euphoric"
- Add physical sensation words: "heavy", "floating", "pulsing", "flowing"
